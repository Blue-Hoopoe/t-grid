2. Grid breakpoints

2.1 Grid wrap

Unfortunately, t-grid can't wrap when it is displaying as a table. To collapse grid on certain breakpoint, you must add t-break-{breakpoint} class to your grid.

We relay on our grid!
We can't wrap by ourselves :( 
<div class="t-grid t-break-sm">
    <div class="t-row">
        <div class="t-col t-xxs-12">
            We relay on our grid!
        </div>
        <div class="t-col t-xxs-12">
            We can't wrap by ourselves :(
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

2.2 Floats on collapse

When grid is collapsed, it is displaying as a blocks with left floats, just like in a float-grids.

We are the table!
But only to certain point.
Because then w evolve to blocks! 
<div class="t-grid t-break-sm">
    <div class="t-row">
        <div class="t-col t-xxs-6 t-sm-4">
            We are the table!
        </div>
        <div class="t-col t-xxs-6 t-sm-4">
            But only to certain point.
        </div>
        <div class="t-col t-xxs-12 t-sm-4">
            Because then w evolve to blocks!
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
