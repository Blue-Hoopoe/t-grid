3. Alignment

3.1 Grids alignment

One of the pros of t-grid is ability to align. There are three options: you can align content to top, to middle and to the bottom. You can apply alignments by adding class to a whole grid.

I'm up.
And down.
I take a lot of space!
But we will always be on top!
That's right, me too! 
<div class="t-grid t-break-sm t-top">
    <div class="t-row">
        <div class="t-col">
            I'm up.
            <br/> And down.
            <br/> I take a lot of space!
        </div>
        <div class="t-col">
            But we will always be on top!
        </div>
        <div class="t-col">
            That's right, me too!
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

3.2 Single cells alignment

By default, all grids are aligned to the middle. You can change just one cell alignment by adding right classes.

Look,
by default I will be always
in the middle.
But I want to stick to the bottom.
And I don't. 
<div class="t-grid t-break-sm">
    <div class="t-row">
        <div class="t-col">
            Look,
            <br/> by default I will be always
            <br/> in the middle.
        </div>
        <div class="t-col t-bottom">
            But I want to stick to the bottom.
        </div>
        <div class="t-col t-top">
            And I don't.
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
