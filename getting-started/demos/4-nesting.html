4.1 Filled nesting

To force filled grids you must provide jQuery, t-grid.js file and create an isntance of a tGrid class just before body ending tag. For grid that should be nested, you must add t-fill class.

We will fill our parent...
...with love and space!

<script src="t-grid.js"></script>
